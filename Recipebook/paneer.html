Paneer Butter Masala

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, garam masala, and turmeric powder. Cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates.
  5. Add the cashew paste and cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes, salt, and kasuri methi. Mix well.
  7. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the curry reaches the desired consistency.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with naan or rice.
