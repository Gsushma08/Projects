Gulab Jamun

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, baking soda, and enough milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small, smooth balls.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Fry the dough balls on low-medium heat until they turn golden brown.
  5. In a separate pan, combine sugar and water to make a sugar syrup. Add saffron strands and cardamom powder. Heat until the syrup thickens slightly.
  6. Drop the fried balls into the warm sugar syrup. Allow them to soak for a few hours
  7. Garnish with chopped nuts if desired.
